Tally Services

Real-Time Cargo Counting & Recording

Our tally clerks meticulously count, record, and verify cargo during loading and unloading. By maintaining accurate logs, we prevent discrepancies between actual and documented shipment quantities, ensuring transparency and accountability. Our expertise reduces errors, loss claims, and supply chain inefficiencies.

Cargo Discrepancy Prevention & Reporting

We ensure cargo integrity by identifying shortages, damages, or unauthorized substitutions in real time. Our tallying process integrates detailed documentation, cross-checking manifests, and generating digital reports for seamless logistics operations. Clients benefit from reduced financial risks and improved supply chain visibility.
