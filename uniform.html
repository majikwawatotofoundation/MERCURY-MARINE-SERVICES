Creating a Sense of Belonging

Dress for Success (Sare za Shule)

In Swahili, "Sare za Shule" means more than just uniforms—it symbolizes belonging. Unfortunately, some children feel excluded in school because their families can’t afford uniforms. Together, we can ensure no child feels left out. Let’s help them feel part of the group.

Empower with Pride

A school uniform does more than clothe a child—it builds confidence, fosters inclusion, and allows them to focus on what matters most: learning. By giving a uniform, you’re giving a child the opportunity to walk into school with pride and hope for a brighter future.
