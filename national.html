A Dream Beyond Reach

In Kenya, attending a National secondary school is a prestigious milestone. For Abdi, a bright boy from a humble family in Mombasa, this dream seemed impossible. His mother sold food on the streets, and his father worked as a fisherman. Frequent fee arrears often kept him out of class, threatening his education.

A Journey to Excellence

With Milele Vision Foundation's support, Abdi overcame the odds and stayed in school. His hard work earned him a spot at Maranda National School, where he became Kenya's top Kiswahili student. Now, he dreams of becoming a celebrated author like Wallah bin Wallah. Together, we can empower more children like Abdi to achieve greatness.
